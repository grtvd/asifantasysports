8. Scoring

The following scoring system will be used:
SKATERS
Goals 15 points
Assists 15 points
Penalty Minutes 2 points
Shots 1 point
Plus/Minus +/-10 points
Bonus Points (added to above)
Power Play Goals +15 points
Power Play Assists +15 points
Short Handed Goals +15 points
Short Handed Assists +15 points
Game Winning Goals +15 points
Game Tying Goals +15 points
Overtime Goals +15 points
Hat Tricks +45 points
Skaters will not received points from Goalie statistics.
GOALIES
Wins 40 points
Losses -40 points
Ties 20 points
Goals Against -15 points
Saves 2 points
Shutouts 120 points
Goalies will not received points from Skater statistics.
