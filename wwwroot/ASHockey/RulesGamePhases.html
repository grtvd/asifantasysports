5. Game Phases

WWFH is divided into 5 phases: Pre-Draft, Draft, Preseason, Season and Playoffs.

Before a league is closed, it is in Pre-Draft phase. Pre-Draft phase gives you, the Owner, time to organize your player rankings for the Draft. Once a league is closed, it will be assigned a Draft date. The Draft date will set for the next available time at 7:00 AM ET. This means if your league is closed on a Thursday at 10:00 AM ET, your first Draft day will be Friday at 7:00 AM ET. If your league closed on a Friday at 2:00 AM ET, your first Draft day will be that same Friday at 7:00 AM ET.

The earliest Draft for any league will be September 24, 1999 at 7:00 AM ET. If your league closes before this date, you will have a longer wait for the Draft.

The Draft phase is 4 days (more on this below).

Preseason phase gives you time to set up your starting lineup for the first week's regular season game. In addition, you may add/drop players through waivers. The Preseason phase may vary in length. Leagues closed on or before September 27, 1999 at 7:00 AM ET will receive 3 days of Preseason. Leagues closed after this time will receive a shortened, if any, Preseason.

The Regular Season lasts 27 weeks and includes 27 games. All weeks except the first week begin on Mondays and end on Sundays and include 7 days of profressional games. The first week of the Season begins on Friday, October 1, 1999 and includes 9 days of professional games.

Playoffs begin the Monday immediately following the last regular season game, April 9, 2000, and last 2 weeks in length.
