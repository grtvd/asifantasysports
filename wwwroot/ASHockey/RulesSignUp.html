Rules
2. Sign-Up

Participation in WWFH involves setting up a Member Account with Affinity Sports and creating a new WWFH Team.

Sign ups are submitted via the Internet at www.affinitysports.com and via fax at (610) 783-4637. For fax sign-up, printout the Sign Up page, fill in all fields and fax in the page. Incomplete sign ups will be rejected. Successful sign ups will be notified via an email welcome letter.

A Team has a one-time cost of $14.95 for new Affinity Sports customers and $12.95 for existing Affinity Sports customers. Additional Teams cost $12.95. Multiple Teams must be signed up under a single Member Account. Existing Affinity Customers must use their existing Member Account. All game transactions are unlimited and free of charge. You may purchase as many Teams as you like.

Any one individual who sets up a second Member Account will be found in violation of these rules and will be disqualified from the Contest and access to all teams will be revoked.

A valid Master Card or VISA credit card is required for sign up.

When signing up for WWFH, you will be asked for the following personal information: full name, address, city, state, zip code and email address. An email address is required for participation in WWFH. Invalid or incomplete information may void you from receiving any Contest Prizes. This information will be kept in strict confidentiality. It will only be used for WWFH. It will not be sold to other companies for purposes of sales or marketing.

For your security, WWFH uses Secured Sockets Layer (or SSL) to transmit your personal information, including your credit card number, across the Internet. This is an industry wide standard for passing encrypted data across the Internet. Your information will be unreadable by outsiders. Your information is completely secure.

If you are uncomfortable about using your credit card over the Internet, please sign-up via fax.

Do not email your credit card information to Affinity Sports. This is not a secure method for transferring your credit card number.

During sign-up, you will be asked for a User Name and a User Password. Write down these two pieces of information. They will be used to log into the game. Your User Name and Password will also be required to receive multi-team discounts. If you lose your password, you will be required to provide us with a piece of personal information in order for Affinity Sports to inform you of it.

As an Owner of a WWFH Team, you will be required to supply three pieces of information for each Team that you purchase; Team City, Team Nickname and Owner Name. The Team City, combined with the Team Nickname, will form your team name. The Owner Name will be the name that others in your league identify you by. An example of a complete team name would be the Philadelphia (Team City) Dragons (Team Nickname) which could be owned by Uncle Rocky (Owner Name). Affinity Sports reserves the right to change the Team City, Team Nickname or Owner Name if any are considered to be in poor taste.
