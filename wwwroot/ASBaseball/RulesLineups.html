10. Starting Lineups

Owners will be required to designate a starting lineup before each game. For the first week, each Owner's starting lineup will be set automatically based on the order his players were picked in the draft. Each Owner's lineup is maintained from week to week. If an Owner does not make any changes, the previous week's lineup will be used.

An Owner's starting lineup must consist of players at the following positions:
1 First Baseman
1 Second Baseman
1 Shortstop
1 Third Baseman
1 Catcher
3 Outfielders
1 Designated Hitter
5 Starting Pitchers
4 Relief Pitchers
If an Owner is unable to start players at these positions, then they will be at a disadvantage. An Owner will not receive any points for any positions that remain open.

Any non-Pitching player may be used to fill the Designated Hitter slot in the lineup.

Starting Lineups will be "locked" at 5:00 AM ET every Monday.

Trades and Waivers (see below) are processed everyday but any roster move that contains a "locked" player will be postponed until after the player is "unlocked".
