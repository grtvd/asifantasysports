Rules
8. Scoring

The following scoring system will be used:
BATTERS
Singles 1 point
Doubles 2 points
Triples 3 points
1-Run Home Runs 4 points
2-Run Home Runs 6 points
3-Run Home Runs 8 points
Grand Slams 10 points
Hit For Cycle 10 points
Runs Scored 2 points
Runs Batted In (RBI) 2 points
Sacrifices 1 point
Hit By Pitch 1 point
Walks 1 point
Strikeouts -1 point
Stolen Bases 1 point
Caught Stealing -1 point
Errors -1 point
Grounded Into Double Play -1 point
Pitchers will not receive points for batting.
PITCHERS
Wins 10 points
Losses -10 points
Saves 5 points
Holds 5 points
Complete Games 10 points
Shutouts 10 points
No Hitters 10 points
Perfect Games 10 points
Outs Pitched 1 point
Singles Allowed -1 point
Doubles Allowed -2 points
Triples Allowed -3 points
1-Run Home Runs Allowed -4 points
2-Run Home Runs Allowed -6 points
3-Run Home Runs Allowed -8 points
Grand Slams Allowed -10 points
Unearned Runs Allowed -1 point
Earned Runs Allowed -2 points
Sacrifices Allowed -1 point
Hit Batsmen -1 point
Walks -1 point
Strikeouts 1 point
Wild Pitches -1 point
Balks -1 point
Errors -1 points
Batters will not receive points for pitching.
