After the Draft, your league will move into the season phase.  Each team will be assigned a head-to-head schedule for the season.

As games are completed during the season, the results will be added to the column on the right.
Each week, you will need to set your starting lineup. After the draft, your lineup will default based on the order your players were drafted.

For a particular week, if you do not make any changes, your previous week's lineup will be used.

The "Show Lineup For" drop menu can be used to see the lineups of other teams in your league.
