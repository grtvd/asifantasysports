OK, so the draft did not go exactly as planned and you tanked the first two games. Looks like it is time for some roster moves. Luckily, WWFB gives you just the tools you need.

The Free Agent page is really more of a general research page. Not only can you scout for free agents with the same searching and sorting abilities as the Draft Rankings page, but you can also include every player in the league.  This is a great way to compare your players with the FA pool as well as other teams' players.
Not many quality players left in the free agent pool? Not to worry. Trade away an extra outfielder for that starting pitcher you so desperately need.

The Trade page is where it all happens.
