Welcome to World Wide Fantasy Baseball Demo!

After sign-up, your first challenge will be the Draft. Many consider the draft to be the most important part of fantasy game playing. Teams with a strong draft tend to do well throughout the season.

To prepare for the draft, you will need to set your draft rankings. This is done on the Draft Rankings page. WWFB provides you with a default draft ranking.

Select a player and then use the <Up> and <Down> buttons to move the player in the ranking. The red <X> button will delete the player from the ranking.   You will need to "Scout" players not already in the ranking.
When you first enter the draft rankings page, the Eligible Players list will be empty. Hitting the <Scout> button opens the Scout Players window.
The Scout Players window is one of the most powerful features of WWFB. It allows you to search and sort on players using 47 different statistical categories across 7 different time periods (last 2-weeks, last 4-weeks, last 8-weeks, season to date, last year, two years ago and three years ago). Up to 3 stats can be selected at once.
Players can be selected using any combination of positions. If desired, the search can be narrowed to a single MLB team or a player's last name.  Once the desired criteria is entered, pressing <Scout> performs the search and returns to the Draft Rankings page.
Finally, back on the Draft Rankings page, the selected players can be sorted by any of the 3 stats selected and then moved into your draft ranking.
