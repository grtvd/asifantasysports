1. Introduction

World Wide Fantasy Baseball (WWFB) (Contest) is the Internet's best Fantasy Baseball game. It is a skill contest in which players (Owners) manage a fantasy team consisting of players from Major League Baseball. Draft players; set your starting line-ups; make trades; perform all the tasks that an MLB manager, general manager, and owner perform. Your ultimate goal will be to win the overall championship and take home the Grand Prize of US$3,000.

Affinity Sports, Inc. is the producer and host of WWFB.
