9. Standings

Owners will be ranked in their division by Winning percentage. Winning percentage is calculated as Wins/(Total Games - Ties). For example, if an Owner's record is 5-4-1, their winning percentage is .555. If their record is 5-5, their winning percentage is .500. If their record is 6-4, their winning percentage is .600.

2 Owner Tie-Breakers will be decided as follows:
1. Winning percentage in head to head competition.
2. Total points in head to head competition.
3. Total points.
4. Winning percentage in divisional competition.
5. Total points in divisional competition.
6. Coin flip.
3 or more Owner Tie-Breakers will be decided as follows:
1. Total points.
2. Winning percentage in divisional competition.
3. Total points in divisional competition.
4. Coin flip.
