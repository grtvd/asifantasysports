11. Waivers

Any player that is not owned can be added to a team via the Waiver process. Owners may claim a player at anytime. Waivers are processed each morning to determine if an Owner received the player that he claimed. If multiple Owners have claimed the same player, the Owner higher in the waiver order will receive the player. After the Draft, the waiver order is the inverse order of the first draft round. When an Owner's claim is processed, the Owner will move to the bottom of the waiver order. The waiver order will be reset each week to the inverse of the league's overall standings.

When submitting a claim, WWFB will always require that a player is selected for release, even if the Owner's roster has fewer than 12 players. When the claim is actually processed, WWFB will only release the player if the roster has more than 12 players.

Any new player that has been added to the waiver pool can not be claimed until he has spent 72 hours in the waiver pool. This allows all Owners to have a fair chance at putting a claim in for a player.

FAIR PLAY: One Owner releasing a player for the purpose of another team claiming that player is collusion and is prohibited. Any Owner found in violation of this will be disqualified from the Contest and access to all teams will be revoked.
