3. Prizes

Prizes will be awarded at the end of the Contest to the Teams that have accumulated the highest overall point totals during the regular season. Playoff points do not count towards overall point totals.

The Prizes will be awarded as follows:
1st US$1,000
2nd   US$500
3rd   US$250
4th   US$100
5th   US$50
6th - 10th   US$25
In the event of a tie at any prize level, prizes will be divided evenly among those who have tied.

All League Winners will receive a WWFB T-shirt. League Winner will be awarded to the Team in the League who wins the League Championship.
