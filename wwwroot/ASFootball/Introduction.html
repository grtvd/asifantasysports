Introduction
World Wide Fantasy Football is the Internet's best on-line Fantasy Football game. Draft players; set starting line-ups; claim free agents; make trades; perform all the tasks that an NFL coach, general manager, and owner perform.

Your ultimate goal will be to win the overall championship and take home the Grand Prize of $1,000.
Participants play in a fantasy league where real NFL players are drafted to build fantasy teams. Fantasy teams then participate in a 17-week head-to-head schedule against other fantasy teams using real stats from the National Football League.

The top four fantasy teams move on to the playoffs for a two-week, single elimination tournament. The team who wins the playoffs is crowned League Champion.
Before the draft and throughout the season, it will be important to have a team at peak performance. To do this, you will need to start off with a strong draft and follow through with smart free agent claims and trades.

To make smart roster moves, it will be important to do good research. WWFF offers the best research capabilities of any fantasy sports game.

WWFF gives you the ability to search and sort on players using 25 different statistical categories across 7 different time periods (last 2-weeks, last 4-weeks, last 8-weeks, season to date, last year, two years ago and three years ago).

WWFF also allows comparing players currently on your roster and on other teams' rosters against players in the free agent pool. It makes it easy to find those sleepers out there.
So go ahead, sign-up for WWFF and enjoy a great season of fantasy football while at the same time competing for the Grand Prize of $1,000.

Good Luck!
