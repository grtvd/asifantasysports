Rules
7. Game Resolution

WWFF is based on weekly head-to-head competition. Each Owner will be assigned a schedule for the regular season. This schedule will consist of weekly games against other Owners in their league. Owners will play division opponents more often than non-division opponents.

An Owner's score for a game consists of the points produced by the players in the Owner's starting lineup. An NFL player's real-life statistics for that week will be used. If an Owner starts a player or defensive team who is injured or is not scheduled to play, that player/team will not contribute any points for the week.

The Owner with the most points for the week wins the game. The more wins an Owner has, the better chance that he will reach the playoffs. Ties will be counted as ties in the standings.

Final game results will be available each Tuesday following the weekend's games, sometime after 7:00 AM ET. In-progress game results will be available the day after the first game of the week.
