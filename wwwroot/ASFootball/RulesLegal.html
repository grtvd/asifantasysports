15. LEGAL RESTRICTIONS

15.1 WWFF IS INTENDED FOR PARTICIPATION OF UNITED STATES CITIZENS ONLY AND SHALL ONLY BE CONSTRUED AND EVALUATED ACCORDING TO UNITED STATES LAW. PERSONS RESIDING IN OTHER JURISDICTIONS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO WIN ANY PRIZES IN CONNECTION WITH THIS CONTEST. THIS CONTEST IS VOID WHERE PROHIBITED BY LAW.

15.2 Winner Selection

WWFF IS A CONTEST OF SKILL. WINNERS WILL BE DETERMINED BY THE OBJECTIVE CRITERIA SET FORTH BELOW. From all eligible entries received, the winners will be determined by the participant who uses his or her skill and knowledge of the relevant football and/or fantasy football rules to accumulate the most points during the WWFF season according to the Official Rules. All entries, along with all copyright, trademark and other proprietary rights associated therewith, become property of Affinity Sports, Inc. (hereafter "ASI") upon submission, and will not be returned to any entrant.

Prize winners will be formally notified the week of January 31, 2000. Winners of US$100 or more may be asked to sign and return an affidavit of eligibility and a liability/publicity release within ten (10) days following attempted winner notification. These forms must be completed and returned within the time period specified or the prize will be forfeited. Prizes will be awarded the week of February 14, 1999. Winners are responsible for any and all taxes on their prizes.

No substitution or transfer of prize permitted.

15.3 PRIZES

One (1) Grand Prize winner will receive US$1,000. One (1) Second Place winner will receive US$250. One (1) Third Place winner will receive US$100.

League Winners will receive WWFF T-shirt. One (1) League Winner per League.

No more than the listed prizes will be awarded.

15.4 Eligibility

WWFF is open to legal residents of the United States. Persons residing in any other jurisdictions may enter (unless this contest or entry into this contest is prohibited by applicable local law), but will not be eligible to win any prizes. Contest entrants must be 18 years of age or older. All federal, state and local laws and regulations apply. Directors, officers and employees of ASI, their distributors, divisions and subsidiaries, licensed retailers, their immediate families, and directors, officers and employees of the contest's promotional partners are not eligible to win any prizes.

WWFF may not be used in any form of gambling. In the event that the award of any prizes to winners of the Contest is challenged by any legal authority, ASI reserves the right in its sole discretion to determine whether or not to award such prizes and whether or not to refund previous payments made by participants.

In the event of a dispute about the identity of any entrant, entries made online will be declared made by the authorized account holder of the e-mail address submitted at the time of entry. "Authorized account holder" is defined as the natural person who is assigned to an e-mail address by an Internet access provider, online service provider or other organization that is responsible for assigning e-mail addresses for the domain associated with the submitted e-mail address. A potential winner may be required to provide ASI with proof that the potential winner is the authorized account holder of the e-mail address associated with the winning entry.
