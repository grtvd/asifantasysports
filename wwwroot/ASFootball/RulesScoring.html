8. Scoring

The following scoring system will be used:
PASSING
Touchdowns 3 points
Touchdown Length (per play)
0 - 9 yds 0 points
10 - 19 yds 1 point
20 - 29 yds 2 points
30 - 39 yds 3 points
40 - 49 yds 4 points
50+ yds 5 points
Total Yardage
0 - 49 yds 0 points
50 - 99 yds 1 point
100 - 149 yds 2 points
150 - 199 yds 3 points
200 - 249 yds 4 points
and so on...

Interceptions

-3 points
Two point conversions 2 points
RUSHING / RECEIVING
Touchdowns 6 points
Touchdown Length (per play)
0 - 9 yds 0 points
10- 19 yds 1 point
20 - 29 yds 2 points
30 - 39 yds 3 points
40 - 49 yds 4 points
50+ yds 5 points
Rushing Yards
0 - 24 yds 0 points
25 - 49 yds 1 point
50 - 74 yds 2 points
75 - 99 yds 3 points
100 - 124 yds 4 points
and so on...
Receiving Yards
0 - 49 yds 0 points
50 - 74 yds 1 point
75 - 99 yds 2 points
100 - 124 yds 3 points
and so on...
Fumbles -3 points
Two point conversions 2 points
KICKING
Successful FG 3 points
Successful PAT 1 point
Unsuccessful FG -3 points
Unsuccessful PAT -1 point
Field Goal Yardage (per play)
0 - 39 yds 0 points
40 - 49 yds 1 point
50 + yds 2 points
DEFENSIVE
Yards Allowed
0 - 149 yds 4 points
150 - 199 yds 3 points
200 - 249 yds 2 points
250 - 299 yds 1 point
300 - 349 yds 0 points
350 - 399 yds -1 point
400 - 449 yds -2 points
450 - 499 yds -3 points
500+ yds -4 points
Sacks 1 point
Safety 2 points
Turnover 2 points
Touchdown
(Return Interception/Fumble)		 6 points
Blocked kick
(Punt, FG or PAT)		 2 points
Players returning kicks for touchdowns will receive touchdown bonus points. They will not receive any credit towards total receiving/rushing yardage. For example, Deion Sanders returns a punt 72 yards for a touchdown. He would receive 11 points (6 for the touchdown plus a bonus 5 points for 50+ yard touchdown). All players are eligible for touchdown points. If Chris Boniol catches a 5 yard pass for a TD on a fake field goal attempt, he would receive 6 points.
