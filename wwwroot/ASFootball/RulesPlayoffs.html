13. Playoffs

A total of 4 Owners, the top 2 Owners from each division, make the playoffs. The second place Owner in the one division plays the first place Owner in the other division.

For each playoff game, statistics from randomly chosen games from the regular season will be picked for each player in an Owner's lineup. These statistics will be used to determine the outcome of the game. NFL playoff statistics are not used in WWFF playoffs.

4 weeks of a player's statistics are averaged to produce a simulated week of play.

For example, if an Owner had Brett Favre as their quarterback in the first playoff game and Favre played 14 of the NFL's 16 game season. WWFF would randomly chose statistics from 4 of the 14 games in which Favre played. The statistics from those games would be used for the playoff game. A player is considered to have played in a game if WWFF has received statistics for the player in the particular game.

A single player's total points for the game selected will be divided by 4 to simulate a single week of play (average total points). The average total points for each player in the lineup will be totaled to determine the total points for Owner's Team. The Owner of with the greater total points will be deemed winner of the game.

The two winners of the first round of playoff games will move onto the second playoff round. The winner of the second playoff round will be deemed League Champion.
